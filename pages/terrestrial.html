Earth

Earth, the third planet from the Sun, is a haven for life, boasting a diameter of approximately 12,742 kilometers and a mass of about 5.97 x 10^24 kilograms. Its surface, covering approximately 71% water and 29% land, hosts a diverse range of ecosystems vital for sustaining biodiversity. Earth's atmosphere, primarily composed of nitrogen and oxygen, shields life from harmful solar radiation and moderates its climate. While Earth faces environmental challenges from human activity, its significance as a unique and habitable world underscores the importance of preserving its delicate balance for future generations.
